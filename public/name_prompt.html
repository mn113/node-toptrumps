Game instructions
  • When the next round starts you will immediately be included, and dealt 25 cards.
  • If nobody else is online, you will play against the computer.
  • When it's your turn, your country card will have blue links and you will see a timer bar on the card.
  • When it's someone else's turn, your card will be greyed out. You just have to wait.
  • You can leave and rejoin the game at any time (like pausing, but play will continue between other players).
