About this page

Top Trumps is a turn-based card game where you win rounds - and cards - by picking the category from the card in front of you which you think will beat your opponent's card.

This online version uses cards generated from the CIA World Factbook - 243 countries and territories to be precise.

You might win a round with Russia by playing its area, New Zealand by playing its longitude, or Vatican City by playing its population density. Get the idea?

This game is written entirely in Javascript and was created for a 48-hour coding competition - although it took a little longer than that to get finished!

Enjoy, and please send any feedback to toptrumps@politestudio.co.uk.
